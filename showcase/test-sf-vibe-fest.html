SF VIBE FEST · OCT 25–31, 2027 · SAN FRANCISCO

The future gets a neighborhood

A seven-day citywide convergence of capital, code, culture, music, art, and civic imagination. Anchored at Moscone. Distributed across museums, waterfronts, civic plazas, cultural institutions, startup spaces, and seven neighborhood block parties.

San Francisco has always been where the future comes to get a body.

Before the future becomes normal, it comes here strange. Before it becomes infrastructure, it comes here as a scene. Before it becomes an industry, it comes here as music, code, protest, poetry, sex, food, risk, argument, light, fog, and people brave enough to live a little ahead of schedule.

The Beats came here to rewrite consciousness. The psychedelic generation came here to turn music into a portal. Queer liberation changed the moral vocabulary of the world from these streets. Punk, rave, Burning Man, Wired, open source, biotech, mobile, social, climate, crypto, and AI all passed through this same charged geography.

Now the next wave is here. Artificial intelligence is not just another technology cycle — it is the new nervous system of capital, culture, media, labor, health, education, cities, and power. And once again, San Francisco is where the wire is thickest.

The public sees the festival.

Seven neighborhood block parties. Museum nights. Public art. Live music. Markets. Free where it counts. Real where it matters. The cultural electricity of the most magnetic week San Francisco has produced in a decade — designed to be walked, not gated.

The business is the room.

A curated Tuesday–Thursday Capital Summit nested inside the seven-day festival. LPs, family offices, GPs, sovereign funds, foundations, and select founders. Application-based. Application-only. White-glove. The room sets the future before it becomes consensus.

Seven nights. Seven neighborhoods. One civic pulse.

  1. Mon · SoMa / Yerba Buena

    The city of builders. Opening night. The room sets itself.

  2. Tue · Embarcadero / Ferry Building

    Waterfront signal. The Capital Summit opens. Founder/LP first-dinner energy at SHACK15.

  3. Wed · Mission / Calle 24 / Valencia

    Murals, food, future folk culture. The block where AI meets taquería.

  4. Thu · Dogpatch / Pier 70 / Mission Bay

    Industrial culture. Climate, biotech, maker energy. Late-night at the Power Station.

  5. Fri · Fillmore / Japantown / North Beach

    Jazz, literature, design, nightlife. The city's lineage on full volume.

  6. Sat · Castro / Haight / Golden Gate Park

    Liberation, counterculture, public joy. The cultural climax.

  7. Sun · Bayview / India Basin / Ocean

    Civic gratitude. Family day. Closing ritual. The fog comes back in.
